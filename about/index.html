ABOUT SENSE

Found in 1993 and located in Yangjiang City, which benefit from the convenient transportation and the abundant materials. YANGJIANG SENSE INDUSTRIES LIMITED is a modern designer, innovator producer and seller of modern household
and living products.

We have four sub factories specialized in kitchenware and household products:Yangjiang Yuejiang Mold Factory, Yangdong Chaoliang Plastic&Hardware Products Co. Ltd, Yangdong Yuefeng Plastic&Hardware Products Factory and Yangjiang Tongyi Industry&Trade Co. Ltd. With our own mould workshop, all kinds of skilled staff, Sense comprises of a variety of advanced manufacturing machines and tooling equipment including the hydraulic presser, tensile machine, big and small punchers, injection molding machine, silicone machine and spray-painting assembly line. We are capable of providing reliable quality products at competitive pricei with on time delivery and outstanding follow up service.
